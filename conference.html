Developer Conference

Deep technical talks and workshops designed to elevate your engineering capabilities

Conference Overview

The SWEeyam 2026 Developer Conference is designed for engineers who value depth over breadth. This is not a vendor showcase or marketing event—it's a technical gathering where industry leaders, alumni, and academic experts share production engineering insights that you can apply immediately.

We respect your expertise and your time, which is why every session is curated for technical substance.

Target Audience

Student Engineers

Preparing for production-level careers

Industry Professionals

Seeking to stay current with engineering best practices

Alumni

Reconnecting with the technical community

Faculty & Researchers

Exploring industry-academia collaboration

Conference Themes

Keynotes and lightning talks focused on deep engineering narratives:

Production Reliability

Building systems that scale, maintain, and evolve in real-world environments with focus on reliability engineering, performance optimization, and operational excellence.

Privacy & Security by Design

Deep dives into secure coding practices, threat modeling, cryptography, and security-first development workflows that integrate privacy and security throughout the development lifecycle.

Scalable Data & ML Systems

Practical sessions on deploying ML models and data pipelines at scale, model reliability and monitoring, MLOps pipelines, and the challenges of maintaining ML systems in production.

Embedded Reliability

Focus on embedded systems, IoT, and hardware-software integration with emphasis on reliability, real-time systems, and edge computing challenges.

Developer Ergonomics

Discussions on developer experience, tooling, workflow optimization, technical decision-making, and creating environments where engineering excellence thrives.

Session Formats

Keynote Presentations

45-60 minutes

Industry leaders and distinguished alumni deliver talks on major themes, sharing insights from their experience building and operating production systems at scale.

Technical Workshops

90-120 minutes

Hands-on sessions where participants work through practical problems, guided by expert instructors. Workshops include live coding, architecture exercises, and interactive Q&A.

Panel Discussions

60 minutes

Moderated conversations among industry experts, exploring topics from multiple perspectives. Panels encourage audience participation and real-time engagement.

Lightning Talks

10-15 minutes

Quick, focused presentations on specific techniques, tools, or lessons learned. Lightning talks provide diverse perspectives and practical takeaways.

Speaker Types

Industry Engineers

Academic Experts

Researchers and faculty members whose work bridges theory with practice

Open Source Maintainers

Technical Depth Guarantee

We understand that alumni and industry professionals attend conferences expecting substantive technical content, not marketing pitches. Every session at SWEeyam 2026 is vetted for technical depth and practical applicability.

Speakers are selected based on their ability to deliver insights that experienced engineers will value, and workshops are designed to challenge participants regardless of their current expertise level.

Why This Matters to Alumni

As an alum, the SWEeyam conference offers an opportunity to:

  • Stay connected with the technical evolution at your alma mater
  • Share your expertise and establish your thought leadership
  • Mentor the next generation of engineers
  • Discover emerging talent for your organization
  • Reconnect with peers and expand your professional network
  • Contribute to the institution's reputation as a center of engineering excellence
