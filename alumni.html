Alumni & Industry

Shape the next generation of engineers through SWEeyam 2026

Dear SRM University – AP Alumni

You've built careers at the forefront of engineering, solving complex problems and leading technical teams. Now, we invite you to bring that expertise back to your alma mater and shape the next generation of engineers through SWEeyam 2026.

Your involvement is not symbolic—it's foundational. SWEeyam's credibility with industry partners, technical depth, and long-term impact depend on alumni like you who ensure the event reflects the standards and practices that define professional engineering excellence.

Ways to Get Involved

Serve as a Mentor

During the 48-hour hackathon, mentor clinics provide structured opportunities for teams to receive expert guidance.

As a mentor, you'll:
  • Conduct one-on-one and small-group sessions with teams tackling challenges in your expertise area
  • Provide real-time feedback on architecture decisions, code quality, and implementation strategies
  • Help teams understand production engineering considerations they might overlook
  • Share insights from your experience building and operating systems at scale

Time Commitment: 4-8 hours over Days 2-3, with flexible scheduling

Speak at the Conference

Deliver a keynote, lead a technical workshop, or participate in a panel discussion.

Share your expertise on topics such as:
  • Production engineering practices and lessons learned
  • Career development and engineering leadership
  • Emerging technologies and industry trends
  • Building high-performing engineering teams

Time Commitment: Preparation time plus session duration (typically 1-3 hours, including Q&A)

Judge Hackathon Solutions

Evaluate teams' submissions based on production engineering criteria.

As a judge, you'll:
  • Review code quality, architecture decisions, and implementation details
  • Assess solutions against industry-standard evaluation criteria
  • Provide constructive feedback during final demos
  • Identify exceptional talent for potential recruitment

Time Commitment: 6-10 hours over Day 3, including solution review and demo evaluation

Sponsor or Partner

Support SWEeyam 2026 through sponsorship, which provides brand visibility and talent access.

Benefits include:
  • Brand visibility among top engineering talent
  • Priority access to participant portfolios and recruitment opportunities
  • Problem statement authorship for hackathon challenges
  • Speaking opportunities and thought leadership positioning
  • Networking access to other industry partners and alumni

Time Commitment: Contact for detailed sponsorship packages

Recruit Talent

SWEeyam functions as an extended technical interview, allowing you to evaluate candidates' engineering skills.

You'll have:
  • Priority access to participant profiles and portfolios
  • Opportunities to engage with teams during mentor clinics and networking sessions
  • Direct access to winning teams and exceptional performers
  • Code repository access for technical evaluation

Time Commitment: Flexible, based on your recruitment needs

Benefits of Participation

Professional Development

  • Establish thought leadership in the engineering community
  • Expand your professional network
  • Stay current with emerging technologies

Organizational Impact

  • Identify and recruit exceptional talent
  • Build brand recognition
  • Contribute problem statements for innovative solutions

Personal Fulfillment

  • Give back to your alma mater
  • Mentor teams and witness their growth
  • Contribute to building a stronger technical ecosystem

Recognition

Alumni contributors are recognized throughout the event and in post-event communications, showcasing your commitment to engineering excellence and institutional support.
