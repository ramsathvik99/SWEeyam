Our Vision

SWEeyam (Purpose) is designed to solve real engineering problems at production depth while creating direct hiring and internship pathways between high-skill developers and industry. It is a three-day, knowledge-intensive hackathon + developer conference that prioritizes hands-on engineering rigor over show-and-tell demos.

Our strategic goals: accelerate industry–academia co-creation; produce deployable prototypes; build talent funnels; and position SRM-AP as a national innovation hub with international reach.

Why SWEeyam Stands Out

Production-Depth Engineering

Hands-on engineering rigor over show-and-tell demos. Solutions evaluated on code quality, architecture, security, scalability, and maintainability.

Sponsor-Authored Problem Statements

Real-world problems tied to measurable KPIs, ensuring participants solve actual engineering challenges businesses face.

On-Site Technical Evaluation

Rolling evaluations, mentor clinics, and HR touchpoints leading to interviews with industry partners.

Repeatable Open Artifacts

Repos, test plans, benchmark results that outlive the event—deployable prototypes with lasting value.

Direct Hiring Pathways

Companies and industry partners have priority access to exceptional participants with demonstrated production-level skills.
